GDSC NYCU · 2022—2023

GDSC NYCU Yearbook: Learn — Connect — Build — Project

We are the Google Developer Student Clubs chapter at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. Our mission: turn what students learn into solutions that ship. In 2022–2023, we partnered with NTHU, ran a structured workshop track, engaged through Women Techmakers and community mixers, guided Solution Challenge teams and Side Projects, and showcased outcomes at Google I/O Extended Hsinchu. This page consolidates our KPIs, annual timeline, full event lineup, and resource links.
